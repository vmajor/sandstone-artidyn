## Precision in a Small Footprint

For entrepreneurs and modern offices, the **Compact Series** offers a low-footprint entry into high-margin retail without sacrificing the visual theater of robotics. This tier is engineered for spaces where every square meter counts.

### The Engine: Commercial-Grade Quality

At the heart of our compact solution is the premium Barluoto BTB-302 commercial espresso engine, delivering a mouthfeel that rivals specialty human baristas.

*   **Capacity:** High-capacity hoppers suitable for daily operations without constant refilling.
*   **Speed:** Optimized for quick turnover, averaging 51 seconds per cup.
*   **Space Saving:** condensed internal architecture (1300mm width) allows for full-service operation in narrow architectural alcoves.

### The Robot: Collaborative Performance

The system features a high-precision 6-axis collaborative robot arm. Unlike traditional vending machines, the arm mimics human movements to handle cups and delivery, turning a simple transaction into a performance that attracts foot traffic.

<Callout variant="base" title="Key Compact Advantages">
  - **Small Footprint:** Designed to fit into standard vending spaces, office corners, or hospital lobbies.
  - **Superior Milk Foam:** Premium espresso units deliver professional-grade texture.
  - **Smart Operations:** Large touchscreens for local ordering with full app-based remote management.
</Callout>
