## The High-Volume Barista

**The Engine: Swiss Performance** We deploy single or multiple **Eversys Cameo** or **Franke** super-automatic machines. These units are the industry standard for high-volume consistent quality.

*   **Throughput:** Capable of producing up to 175 espressos or cappuccinos per hour per machine.
    
*   **Dual-Milk System:** Supports various milk types with Micro Air Dosing (MAD) for perfect foam texture.
    

**Estimated Investment**

*   **5,000 – 5,000 USD**
    
*   _Price dependent on specific engine configuration (Eversys vs. Franke, additional modules, ice maker, etc.) and telemetry packages._
    

**Ideal Use Cases** When the line is long and customers are in a rush, the High-Volume Barista delivers. It allows operators to turn "off-peak" hours—like 3:00 AM at an airport gate—into revenue-generating hours without staffing costs.

**Key Features**

*   **Speed:** Designed to handle rush-hour volumes that would overwhelm standard baristas.
    
*   **Telemetry:** Deep data integration for real-time consumption analysis and preventative maintenance.
    
*   **24/7 Uptime:** Engineered for continuous operation in high-traffic concession environments.
    

**Special feature**

*   If you have an existing contract with Eversys or Franke, or compatible fully automatic machines we can integrate your own machines with our robot barista system
