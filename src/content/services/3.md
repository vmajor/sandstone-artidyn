## The Craft Coffee Robot Barista Experience

**The Hardware: Authentic Italian Espresso** Unlike the other tiers, this solution uses a **traditional semi-automatic espresso machine** (such as a 2-group La Marzocco) and precision grinders (for example Elektra).

*   **The Process:** The two robot arms perform actual portafilter movements - grinding, tamping, and extracting - just like a human barista.
    
*   **Visuals:** Housed in a premium enclosure often featuring 360° glass or foldable windows for maximum visibility of the process.
    

**Estimated Investment**

*   **From 5,000 USD**
    
*   _Price varies based on espresso machine selection (La Marzocco/custom) and enclosure finish, or no enclosure, additional features, grinders, milk feeders, food serving options, full customization is available_
    

**The Robot: Dual-Arm Coordination** Powered by two **6-axis collaborative robot arms**, the system replicates complex human dexterity.

*   **Latte Art:** The arms are programmed to pour complex latte art patterns, a feature that justifies premium pricing.
    
*   **Collaboration:** One arm manages the extraction while the other steams milk, ensuring optimal workflow and theater.
    

**Ideal Use Cases** Best suited for luxury retail, existing cafe brand franchises, hotel lobbies, and experiential marketing where the "show" is as valuable as the quality of coffee.
